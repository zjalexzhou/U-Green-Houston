Houston Open Green Space Profile

The Houston Open Green Space Profile is a web-based visualization for the capstone research project designed to analyze the current coverage and demand of parks in the City of Houston. Data presented here come from Trust for Public Land, City of Houston GIS Data Hub, Harris County Department of Health, US Department of Agriculture, and US Census Bureau. To download a complete dataset and learn more about this research project, visit https://github.com/zjalexzhou/U-Green-Houston.
